ConnexGrow Content Safety & Compliance Policy

At ConnexGrow, we are committed to maintaining a safe and responsible digital environment. Our policy outlines how we address key safety and compliance issues:

1. Prevention of Child Sexual Abuse & Exploitation (CSAE)

  • Zero-tolerance policy for CSAE-related content
  • AI-powered detection systems for real-time content monitoring
  • Strict age verification measures to protect minors
  • Immediate account termination and reporting to authorities for violators

2. Detection & Removal of Harmful Content

  • Automated content scanning using advanced AI technology
  • Human moderation team for content review and policy enforcement
  • Proactive removal of violent, illegal, or inappropriate content
  • Regular updates to our content filtering systems

3. Reporting Mechanisms

  • Easy-to-use in-app reporting tools for users
  • Swift response system for addressing user reports
  • Prioritized handling of reports related to child safety
  • Transparent appeal process for content removal decisions

4. Compliance with Legal & Regulatory Requirements

  • Adherence to GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant data protection laws
  • Cooperation with law enforcement agencies on legal matters
  • Regular compliance audits and updates to our policies
  • Strict enforcement of our Terms of Service

5. Child Safety Standards

  • Age-appropriate content filters and restrictions
  • Parental controls for monitoring and managing child accounts
  • Educational resources on digital safety for young users
  • Secure communication channels to prevent unauthorized contact
